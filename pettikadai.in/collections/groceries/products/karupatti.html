What is Karupatti?

Karupatti, or palm jaggery, is a natural sweetener made from palm sap (Panai Saaru). It is rich in minerals like calcium, iron, and phosphorus. making it a healthier alternative to white sugar. Perfect for sweets, beverages like tea, coffee, payasam, panagam, etc.,

Health Benefits

Unlike refined sugar, Karupatti boosts energy, aids digestion, and adds a deep caramel-like flavor to your dishes.

How to use Karupatti?

  • As a sugar substitute in coffee, tea, herbal tea, milk, payasam and juices.

  • In desserts like Cakes and Brownies

  • In traditional sweets like Mysorepak, Adhirasam and Kolukattai
